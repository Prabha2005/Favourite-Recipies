Dosa with Potato Filling Recipe

A traditional South Indian breakfast, crispy dosa with spiced potato filling.

Steps

1. Prepare Dosa Batter:

Soak rice, urad dal, and fenugreek seeds in water for 4–6 hours. Grind to a smooth batter, add salt, and let it ferment overnight.

2. Make Potato Filling:

Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chopped green chilies. Sauté onions until golden. Add turmeric powder and mashed potatoes. Mix well. Add salt and garnish with coriander leaves.

3. Cook Dosa:

Heat a flat pan or tawa. Pour a ladle of batter and spread in a circular motion. Drizzle oil around the edges and cook until crispy. Flip if needed.

4. Assemble:

Place a portion of potato filling in the center of the dosa. Fold and serve hot with coconut chutney and sambar.
