Veg Starters Recipes

Delicious and crispy starters to complement your main course.

Recipes

1. Vegetable Pakoras:

Mix gram flour with chopped vegetables, spices, and water. Deep fry small portions until golden and serve hot.

2. Masala Vadai:

Grind soaked chana dal with spices. Mix with onions, curry leaves, and salt. Shape into discs and deep fry.

3. Vegetable Cutlets:

Mix mashed potatoes, vegetables, and spices. Shape into patties, coat with bread crumbs, and shallow fry until golden.

4. Paneer Tikka:

Marinate paneer cubes with yogurt, spices, and ginger-garlic paste. Grill or roast until slightly charred.

5. Crispy Gobi 65:

Blanch cauliflower, dip in a spiced flour batter, and deep fry until crispy. Serve hot with ketchup.
